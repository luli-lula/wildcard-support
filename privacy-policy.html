Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Wildcard ("we", "our", or "the app") is a plant identification and collection app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Photos. When you use the Discover feature, the app captures or selects a photo to identify a plant. The photo is sent to our server for AI-powered identification and is not stored on our server after processing. Your photos are saved locally on your device as part of your collection.

Location. With your permission, the app records the approximate location (city/area level) where you discover a plant. This is stored locally on your device as part of your field notes. Location data is not sent to any third party.

Device Information. When you send feedback via email, basic device information (iOS version, device model) is included to help us diagnose issues. No device identifiers are collected otherwise.

How We Use Your Information

  • Plant identification: Photos are sent to our server, which uses iNaturalist's Computer Vision API and OpenAI to identify plant species. Photos are processed in real-time and not retained.
  • Card content generation: Species names are sent to our server to generate educational card content. No personal data is included in these requests.
  • AI chat: When you use the "Ask More" feature, your conversation messages are sent to our server for AI processing. Conversations are not stored on our server.

Data Storage

All your collection data — plant cards, photos, discovery locations, and badges — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have a user account system and do not store your personal data on any server.

Third-Party Services

  • iNaturalist (privacy policy) — Used for plant species identification via their Computer Vision API, and for reference photos via their public API.
  • OpenAI (privacy policy) — Used for AI-powered plant identification fallback, card content generation, and chat responses.
  • Cloudflare (privacy policy) — Our server infrastructure provider.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Wildcard does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete individual plant cards within the app, or delete all data by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
