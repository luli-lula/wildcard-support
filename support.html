Frequently Asked Questions

How does plant identification work?
Wildcard uses AI-powered image recognition (iNaturalist Computer Vision + OpenAI) to identify plants from your photos. For best results, take a clear photo of the plant's most distinctive feature â€” flowers, leaves, or bark.

Does the app work offline?
Browsing your existing collection works offline. However, identifying new plants and generating card content requires an internet connection.

Where is my data stored?
All your plant cards, photos, and collection data are stored locally on your device. We do not have user accounts or cloud storage. If you delete the app, your collection will be lost.

Is the identification always accurate?
AI identification is a helpful starting point but not always 100% accurate. Always verify with multiple sources before consuming or interacting with any wild plant.

Can I back up my collection?
Your data is included in your iCloud device backup if enabled. We plan to add more backup options in future updates.
